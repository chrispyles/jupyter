insurance.ipynb
This notebook focuses on an insurance data set that has information about premiums, BMI, sex, and smoker vs. non-smoker, among other things. In this notebook, I looked for linear relationships between BMI and insurance premiums using the correlation coefficient and I ran an A/B test to determine whether or not smokers are charged more in insurance premiums than are non-smokers.
avocado.ipynb
In this notebook, I wrote a k-nearest neighbors classifier that classified avocadoes as organic or conventional using their average price, volume sold, and number of bags used. In this way, I got a 7-NN classifier with 95% accuracy, and I showed that the optimal values of k were 7, 13, and 49.
